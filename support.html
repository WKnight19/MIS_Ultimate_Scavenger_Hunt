Getting Started

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button on the login page. You'll need to use your University of Alabama email address (@ua.edu or @crimson.ua.edu).

  1. Go to the Login page
  2. Click "Create Account"
  3. Enter your university email and create a password
  4. Verify your email address
  5. Complete your profile
How do I join a hunt?

You can browse and join available hunts from the Browse Hunts page.

  1. Navigate to "Browse Hunts" in the sidebar
  2. Find a hunt that interests you
  3. Click "Join Hunt" on the hunt card
  4. Start completing tasks!
What are points and how do I earn them?

Points are earned by completing tasks within hunts. Each task has a point value displayed on the task card. Complete tasks before their deadline to earn full points.

  • Submit tasks on time for full points
  • Late submissions may receive reduced points
  • Bonus points are awarded for completing hunts early
  • Team activities can provide extra point opportunities

Troubleshooting

I can't log in to my account

If you're having trouble logging in, try these steps:

  1. Make sure you're using your university email address
  2. Check that Caps Lock is not enabled
  3. Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link
  4. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  5. Try a different browser

If you still can't log in, contact support.

My submission won't upload

If your submission isn't uploading properly:

  • Check your file size (max 25MB per file)
  • Ensure you're using a supported file format
  • Try compressing large images
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try using a different browser
I submitted the wrong file

If you submitted the wrong file, you can resubmit as long as the deadline hasn't passed. Your new submission will replace the previous one. If the deadline has passed, contact your instructor or TA for assistance.

Policies & Guidelines

Academic Integrity

All submissions must be your own original work. Collaboration is encouraged on team tasks, but individual submissions must reflect your own efforts.
