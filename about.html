An interactive platform designed to engage MIS students with hands-on challenges, foster community, and make learning tangible and fun.

Our Mission

To directly address the need for MIS students to engage with each other, their professors, teaching assistants, and the university in an interactive way.

The project allows students to track tangible progress, complete explicitly defined tasks, and build community outside of the classroom and class time.

Our Vision

To create the most engaging and innovative student experience platform in the MIS program, combining gamification with practical skill-building.

We believe learning happens best when it's active, social, and rewarding.
