Frequently Asked Questions

Help Center Contact Us
How do I create an account?

Click "Sign Up" on the login page and use your University of Alabama email (@ua.edu or @crimson.ua.edu). Verify your email to complete registration.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset link sent to your inbox. The link expires after 24 hours.

Can I change my display name or email?

Yes! Go to your Profile page and click "Edit Profile" to update your display name and other information. Email changes require verification.

How do I join a hunt?

Browse available hunts in the "Browse Hunts" section and click "Join Hunt" on any hunt you'd like to participate in. Some hunts may require instructor approval.

Can I leave a hunt after joining?

Yes, but your progress will be lost. Go to "My Hunts" and click "Leave Hunt." You can rejoin later, but you'll need to start over.

What happens if I miss a deadline?

Late submissions may receive reduced points depending on the hunt settings. Contact your instructor if you have extenuating circumstances.

How do I submit evidence for a task?

Open the task and click "Submit." You can upload files, photos, or provide URLs as evidence. Add notes to explain your submission.

How are points calculated?

Each task has a base point value. You receive full points for on-time submissions that meet requirements. Graders may adjust points based on quality.

How do I earn badges?

Badges are earned by completing hunts, reaching milestones, and maintaining streaks. Each badge has specific unlock criteria shown on your profile.

How often is the leaderboard updated?

The leaderboard updates automatically every hour. You can also manually refresh to see the latest rankings.

What file types can I upload?

We support images (JPG, PNG, GIF), documents (PDF, DOC, DOCX), videos (MP4, MOV), and archives (ZIP). Maximum file size is 25MB.

Why won't my file upload?

Check your file size (max 25MB), file format, and internet connection. Try compressing large files or using a different browser.

Is there a mobile app?

The platform is fully mobile-responsive. Access it through your mobile browser for the best experience. A native app is planned for future release.

Which browsers are supported?

We support the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, keep your browser updated.

Didn't find your answer?

Our support team is ready to help you with any questions not covered here.
